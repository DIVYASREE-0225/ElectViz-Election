# ElectViz-Election

# 🚀 Project Overview

ElectViz – Election Data Visualization for Media is an interactive data visualization platform developed to help media organizations, analysts, and journalists understand complex election data quickly and clearly. During elections, a huge amount of data such as vote counts, party performance, constituency results, and voter turnout is generated from multiple sources. Analyzing this data manually becomes difficult and time-consuming.

To solve this challenge, ElectViz transforms raw election datasets into clear and interactive dashboards that allow users to explore insights easily. The dashboard enables analysis of vote share trends, party and candidate performance, voter turnout patterns, and constituency-level results through visually rich charts and filters. 📊

For this project, we used state-level election data across multiple states to build a structured and scalable dataset for the dashboards. Our team collaborated to collect and organize the election datasets.
✨ Personally, I contributed by collecting and preparing the election data for Assam and Uttar Pradesh, while the remaining state datasets were gathered and organized by other team members.

In addition to the election dashboard, we also developed a Blinkit Product Sales Dashboard 🛒📈 to analyze product sales performance. This dashboard focuses on insights such as product category performance, sales distribution, and business trends, helping stakeholders understand sales data in a simple and visual manner.

Overall, this project demonstrates how data analytics and visualization can convert complex datasets into meaningful insights, enabling faster decision-making and more effective reporting. The dashboards are designed to be scalable, interactive, and adaptable for analyzing future election and business datasets
